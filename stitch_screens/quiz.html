What is the primary function of the mitochondria in a eukaryotic cell?

lightbulb

Explanation

The mitochondria are known as the "powerhouse of the cell" because they generate most of the cell's supply of adenosine triphosphate (ATP), which is used as a source of chemical energy for cellular functions.
